About Camel Spit Studio

Camel Spit Studio is an independent game development studio based in Lisbon, Portugal, founded by passionate developers Diogo Dias and Carlos Correia. As an indie team from Lisbon, Portugal, we focus on creating unique gaming experiences that push the boundaries of creativity and innovation.

Our mission is to develop games that not only entertain but also challenge players to think differently and explore new possibilities in gaming. As a Portuguese indie game studio, we bring a fresh perspective to the gaming industry with our innovative approach to game development.

Based in the vibrant city of Lisbon, Portugal, our indie game development team combines technical expertise with creative vision to create memorable gaming experiences. Our first major project, Battle Bumpers, showcases our commitment to quality and innovation in the indie gaming space.

Battle Bumpers - 3D Vehicular Combat Game

Battle Bumpers: 3D Third-Person Vehicular Combat

Battle Bumpers is an intense arena-based vehicular combat game where players compete to knock opponents off the stage using physics-based bumping mechanics. Built in Unreal Engine 5 with advanced vehicle physics and multiplayer support. As an indie game from Lisbon, Portugal, Battle Bumpers represents our commitment to innovative game design and technical excellence.

Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Platform: Windows PC
Players: Up to 8 players
Development: 4 months
Physics-Based Combat Multiplayer Power-Ups Arena Combat

Core Mechanics

  • Vehicle Physics: Custom APawn with realistic mass and inertia
  • Drifting System: Handbrake mechanics with speed boost rewards
  • Damage System: Progressive damage scaling with knockback multipliers
  • Power-Ups: Saw Blade, Flight, Grappling Hook, Bounce Shield, Mines, Grenades
  • Boost System: Strategic boost collection and usage (max 3)
