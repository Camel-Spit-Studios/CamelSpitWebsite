Battle Bumpers

3D Third-Person Vehicular Combat

Battle Bumpers is an intense arena-based vehicular combat game where players compete to knock opponents off the stage using physics-based bumping mechanics. Built in Unreal Engine 5 with advanced vehicle physics and multiplayer support.

Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Platform: Windows PC
Players: Up to 8 players
Development: 4 months
Physics-Based Combat Multiplayer Power-Ups Arena Combat

Core Mechanics

  • Vehicle Physics: Custom APawn with realistic mass and inertia
  • Drifting System: Handbrake mechanics with speed boost rewards
  • Damage System: Progressive damage scaling with knockback multipliers
  • Power-Ups: Saw Blade, Flight, Grappling Hook, Bounce Shield, Mines, Grenades
  • Boost System: Strategic boost collection and usage (max 3)
